TEC MEMBERship

Unlike most associations, TEC membership is currently free and open to anyone, globally.

New members are typically invited to join The Collective by an existing member. Our only ask is that every member share their professional contact information and be open to networking via LinkedIn outside of the monthly meetings.

Our meetings are typically held via video conference, in English, anytime between 7-9pm EST for 60-90 mins. We also maintain a LinkedIn Group where members can share their concerns, comments, questions and suggestions with other members, at any time. 

TEC members range in level of professional seniority and include Directors, Vice Presidents, C-Suite Executives, Retirees and Students. There is no pressure to speak in the online meetings but offline collaboration and networking within the group is highly encouraged. 

The Executive Collective recognizes and nurtures respect, vulnerability and trust as the cornerstones of our healthy, productive and supportive community. These are gifts we exchange with each other to our mutual benefit. 

Come grow with us!
